Solving real problems.

We interviewed ten local economy officers. Three problems came up repeatedly:

  • Who's active is unclear. Registers list far more entries than there are businesses actually active.
  • Rules are scattered across local, provincial, Flemish and federal sources.
  • Knowledge is undocumented, scattered across inboxes and individuals.

What gets built is shared with all 58 local governments of the province.

More than coders.

Registration is open to builders, designers, operators and domain experts. The strongest teams combine technical depth with practical municipal judgement.
